The Real Problem

Most people have no idea where their money actually goes.

That's not a character flaw — it's a tool problem. Budgeting apps are either too complex and time-consuming, or so generic they never fit your real life. So most people just… don't budget at all.

You open your bank account at the end of the month, feel vaguely stressed, and wonder where it all went. Sound familiar? It doesn't have to be this way.

Clarity doesn't require a finance degree. It doesn't require hours of setup. It just requires the right tool — one that fits how you actually live.
