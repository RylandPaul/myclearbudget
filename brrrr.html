The Problem

Most BRRRR deal analyses are incomplete.
Or just wrong.

The numbers that look good on the surface often hide the problems underneath. Here's what most investors miss.

🔨

Rehab estimates are too low

Electrical, HVAC, plumbing, permits — the real numbers are 30-50% higher than most first estimates. When the actual bill hits, the deal breaks.

📉

ARV is optimistic, not realistic

The bank's appraiser uses different comps than you do. If your deal only works at one specific ARV, it doesn't work.

💸

Holding costs aren't modeled

Every month in rehab costs money — taxes, insurance, utilities, loan interest. A 3-month job that runs 6 months can add $8-12k you didn't budget.

🏦

Refinance math is assumed, not calculated

At 7%+ rates, the refi payment changes everything. The deal that worked at 4.5% may be cash-flow negative today. You need to model the actual number.
