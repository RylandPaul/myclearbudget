What's Inside the Guide

Five moves you can make this week — with or without MyClearBudget.

01

Write Down Every Account

Make your financial picture concrete. Banks, credit cards, loans, retirement — all of it in one list.

02

Track Last Week's Spending

Pull up your bank app and record every transaction from the last 7 days. Don't judge it — just see it.

03

Set ONE Savings Goal

Not five. Pick your most important goal, set a target date, and calculate your monthly saving requirement.

04

Cancel One Subscription

Find one recurring charge you barely use and cancel it today. That's $120–$240/year back in your pocket.

05

Set Up a Basic Budget

It doesn't have to be perfect — it just has to exist. A rough plan beats no plan every single time.
